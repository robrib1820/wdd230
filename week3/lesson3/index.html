Our purpose

We aim to strengthen and consolidate commercial and economic relations between Brazil and Japan, in order to assist in the development of commerce and industry in the State of Paraná through Japanese investments in the region. The Chamber of Commerce and Industry Brazil Japan of Paraná brings together members of different profiles and sizes, sectors and cities, which together give strength, representation and sustainability to the institution, helping to consolidate our mission, which is to economically integrate both countries in Paraná.
